Abhay Deol: Why creativity should win against business

A man's fight to prove that he is not dead, a woman's silent protest to end her life for a cause - these are but a few stories waiting to be told. In this talk recorded at TEDIndia, actor Abhay Deol says there are so many stories to tell in India, but often in the business of filmmaking, commercial considerations overtake the art of good storytelling.

 

About the Speaker

Abhay Deol
Actor


Indian film actor Abhay Deol, often hailed as a “thinking actor” in the Hindi movie circles, has appeared in popular films such as Dev D and Oye Lucky! Lucky Oye!

Abhay is a self-described fan of "cinema that has the 'real' element". He feels movies can be entertaining as well as socially conscious. He recently turned producer and hopes to make films that are relevant.
